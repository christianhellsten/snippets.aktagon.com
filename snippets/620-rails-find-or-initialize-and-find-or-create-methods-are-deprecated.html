Rails find_or_initialize and find_or_create methods are deprecated

The find\_or\_initialize and find\_or\_create methods have been deprecated in Rails x.x.x. This is what you need to do: ```ruby Before: User.find_or_create_by_name('XXX') After: User.find_or_create_by(name: 'XXX') Before: User.find_or_initialize_by_name('XXX') After: User.where(name: 'XXX').first_or_initialize ``` If you want the old methods, see .
