How to hide the status bar in a Phonegap iOS app

To hide the status bar in a phonegap project: 1. Double click on your project, or .plist, in XCode 2. Select the tab named "Info" 3. Add a new property by selecting an item in the list and then clicking the plus sign 4. Name the property "View controller-based status bar appearance" 5. Set the property value to "NO" Next: 1. Select the tab named general 2. Check the "Hide status bar" located under "Deployment Info" Done.
