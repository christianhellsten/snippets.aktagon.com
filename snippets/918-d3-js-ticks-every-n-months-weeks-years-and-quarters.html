D3.js ticks every n months, weeks, years, and quarters

Use D3.js ranges to display ticks every n months, weeks, years, and quarters: ```javascript var xAxis = d3.axisBottom() xAxis.tickValues(d3.timeMonths(firstQtr, lastQtr, 4)) ``` Reference: https://github.com/d3/d3-time#ranges
