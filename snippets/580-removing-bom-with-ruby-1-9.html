Removing BOM with Ruby 1.9

Removing the BOM with Ruby 1.9: ```ruby body = File.open(file, "r:bom|utf-8").read() File.open(file, 'w') do |xass| xass << body end ``` In Ruby 1.8 you could do something like this: ```ruby body = body[3..-1] if body[0..2] == "\357\273\277" ```
